Biography

Milton Parron Padovan is an Agricultural Technician, Biologist and General Biology Specialist, with an emphasis on Ecology. He holds a PhD in Agronomy, with an emphasis in Soil Science, concentrating his academic training in Agroecology and Organic Production. He is also Post-Doctor in Agroecology, with emphasis on Diversified Agroforestry Systems.

He has been working as a professional since 1985 (31 years), with experiences in rural extension, research, teaching, management and consulting.
