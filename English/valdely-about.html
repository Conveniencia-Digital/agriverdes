Biography

Professor of the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Amazonas, Campus Manaus-East Zone (IFAM-CMZL) and Founder-Curator of the EAFM Herbarium of this institute. Teacher and advisor accredited in the Graduate Program in Botany of INPA. He acts in the research and dissemination of PANC – Non-Conventional Food Plants. She has experience in Botany, with emphasis on Economic Botany, Taxonomy of Phanerogams, Ethnobotany, Herbarium and Biodiversity, working mainly on the following topics: unconventional plant foods, plant genetic resources, food security, floristry, olericultura (unconventional vegetables) and Agroecology. Doctor in Fitotecnia-Horticultura (2007) by PPG Phytotechny of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul – UFRGS. Master’s Degree in Biological Sciences (Botany) by the National Institute of Research of the Amazon – INPA (2002). Graduated in Biological Sciences from the State University of Londrina – UEL (2000) Author of the book Non-Conventional Food Plants (PANC) in Brazil in co- Lorenzi (Plantarum Publishing House, 2014).
