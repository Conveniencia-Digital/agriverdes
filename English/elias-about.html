Biography

Researcher at the Tropical Agronomic Center for Research and Teaching – CATIE, Costa Rica. Forest Engineer from the Federal University of Paraíba – UFPB, Brazil. Master in Agrarian Sciences and Natural Resources with specialization in Multiple Use Forestry at CATIE. PhD in Education from the State University at a Distance – UNED, Costa Rica. He has experience in the area of Rural Development and Agroforestry Systems, working on the themes of: Sustainable Agricultural Production, Participatory Development, Rural Education, Sustainable Coffee Production and Integrated Pest Management.
