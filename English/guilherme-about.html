Biography

Founder and partner of Fluxus Ecological Design, since 2006 integrates his academic training as a civil engineer to the principles of ecological design in the design of innovative systems of integrated water management. Awarded for his projects in Brazil and abroad, he is also an activist, co-idealizer of the Já Cisterna Movement, facilitator of courses for technicians and lay people for the empowerment of people in water care.
