How do I attend to the lectures?

Just sign up with your email right here on this website. Close to the congress start date you will receive in your email the schedule including information about the lecturer, the day and the lecture time along with the link that will be used to enter the conference room where the lecture will be displayed. It is very important after your subscription to register our email as reliable and save it as favorite since only the link of the lecture will be sent by email, preventing you from missing the lecture.

Is the 2nd AgroecoWeb free?

Yes, AgroecoWeb is a free congress. All lectures will be available for free on the days and schedules.

Will the lectures be presented in person?

No. AgroecoWeb is a 100% ONLINE conference and you can watch from the comfort of your home or at work. Therefore, you will not have any type of travel and travel costs like face-to-face conferences and the best without carbon emissions.

Will the lectures be broadcast live?

No. The lectures are recorded in advance by the lecturer to facilitate their agenda and avoid failing the broadcasts. We broadcast these recorded lectures according to the AgroecoWeb programming.
