Biography

Jacimar L. de Souza is a productivity fellow in Technological Development at CNPq. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Agronomy from the Federal University of Espírito Santo (1982), a Ph.D. in Agronomy (Phytotechnology) from the Federal University of Lavras (1985) and a PhD in Plant Engineering from the Federal University of Viçosa (2006). He is currently a researcher at the Capixaba Institute for Research, Technical Assistance and Rural Extension. He has experience in Agronomy, with emphasis in Agroecology, working mainly in the following subjects: Evaluation of cultivars of vegetables, organic matter cycling systems, organic composting, organic crop management, soil studies in organic system, economic and energy assessments Of organic crops. Total publication of 256 technical-scientific works. Author of the books: Organic Agriculture – Technologies for the production of healthy foods, vol. 1 (1998), vol. 2 (2005) and vol. 3 (2015); 5 video-courses on organic farming of vegetables (1999 to 2002)
