Biography

Carlos Armênio Khatounian graduated in Agronomic Engineering from the University of São Paulo (1983), Master in Ecological Agriculture by Landbouwuniversiteit Wageningen (1992) and PhD in Sustainable Agriculture from Iowa State University for Science and Technology (2004). He worked from 1984 to 2007 at the Agronomic Institute of Paraná, Area of ​​Phytotechnology, working in research projects, research – extension and development, in themes associated with unconventional food plants, production systems, ecologically based agriculture, family agriculture and Rural development. In 2007, he transferred to the Luiz de Queiroz College of Agriculture, University of São Paulo, where he is a professor of the Department of Plant Production. He is currently working in the area of ​​Agorecology and Organic Agriculture, working in research and development projects, oriented to the design and management of sustainable production systems, and above all to the teaching of Agroecology.
