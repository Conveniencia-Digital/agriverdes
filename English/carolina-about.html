Biography

Carolina Starr is Consultant on Biodiversity and Ecosystems Services. Ecologist, specialist in Agroecology and ecosystem services and with experience in the area of rural extension and public policies for agroecology. Their activities include strengthening the pollinator agenda and the ecological role they play in food systems, supporting countries in adopting agroecology as a strategy to transform food systems into more sustainable and equitable systems.
